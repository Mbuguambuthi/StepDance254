About

Coach Maxwell, affectionately known as Coach Max, is the visionary founder behind Step Dance 254. With over a decade of experience in fitness and dance instruction, Max is a certified personal trainer passionate about helping people achieve both physical wellness and joyful movement.

His energetic teaching style and dedication to community wellness have inspired countless Nairobian's to embrace step-based workouts as a lifestyle. Through rhythm, sweat, and movement, Coach Max transforms ordinary fitness into a cultural rhythm revolution.
