Step Dance 254 is a high-energy dance fitness program that fuses step aerobics with rhythmic choreography. It’s designed to help participants burn calories, improve coordination, and enjoy every beat — all while working out.

Not at all! Step Dance 254 is beginner-friendly. Whether you're new to fitness or a seasoned dancer, our instructors guide every move, allowing you to progress at your own pace while still getting a great workout.

Come in comfortable workout gear, bring a water bottle, and wear supportive sneakers. We provide the step platforms and an energetic playlist — you bring the vibes and the will to move!

We host both physical sessions across various locations in Nairobi and virtual classes online. You can check our Contact or Services page for details on how to join or book a class.

Inspiring rhythm and movement around the world.
